## 🚩 Problem Statement (Problem ID: 15)
The rapid spread and massive volume of video content in form of YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, and TikToks make it difficult to quickly detect and fact-check misinformation efficiently. Currently, cross-referencing audio and visual claims made in fast-paced videos against factual data is a manual, overwhelming, and technically prohibitive process for the average viewer, allowing fake news and misleading narratives to spread unchecked.
